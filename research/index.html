Research

My interests primarily lie in statistical learning theory and machine learning theory.

My current work is in statistical learning theory in Berkeley's Statistical Machine Learning group (advised by Nikita Zhivotovskiy), deep learning theory at BAIR in the DeWeese lab, and information geometry (generously supported by a grant from VESSL AI). I also work on computational modeling of (\(\alpha\),n) reactions at LLNL.
